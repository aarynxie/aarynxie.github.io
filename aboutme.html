Hello,

I'm Aaryn Xie. I'm a graphic designer and illustrator based in Canada. I'm currently in my second year of Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo. Through my art, I hope to bring more hope and life to this world. I am passionate about connecting people through storytelling in my art. I am especially interested in finding the beauty in the mundane things in life.

When I'm not drawing, I can be found learning about the different languages and cultures around the world.
